Campaigns

Playable campaigns! All files are .pdf files.
    Unox' secrets

    This campaign takes a group of adventurers to the planet Ifreet on which an imperial city called Unox promises riches, experiences and yet unbeknownst technologies.

    It is designed to be more of a jungle adventure, in which the group has to clear various quests in a dangerous and rough area. Taking place more around Unox as actually in the city the adventurers will have to show their will and skills to survive.
    There are a lot of places to find and explore and a lot of choices to make. The story is made as dynamic as possible to give the gamemaster many options to bring in own ideas.
